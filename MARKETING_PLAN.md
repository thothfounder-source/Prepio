# InterviewReady AI Marketing Checklist

## Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Create social media accounts (@InterviewReadyAI)
- [ ] Set up Google Analytics on landing page
- [ ] Write launch announcement for X/Twitter
- [ ] Join 10 relevant Reddit communities
- [ ] Create LinkedIn company page
- [ ] Draft Product Hunt submission

## Week 2: Content Blitz  
- [ ] Post "Building in Public" thread on X
- [ ] Share progress video on LinkedIn
- [ ] Write "Show HN" post for Hacker News
- [ ] Create TikTok account, post app demo
- [ ] Reach out to 5 career coaches for partnerships

## Week 3: Community Engagement
- [ ] Post helpful content in Reddit job communities
- [ ] Join 5 Discord career/job search servers
- [ ] Guest on 2 career-focused podcasts
- [ ] Launch affiliate program for career coaches
- [ ] Submit to startup directories (AngelList, etc.)

## Week 4: Launch Push
- [ ] Product Hunt launch (Tuesday-Thursday)
- [ ] Press release to tech blogs
- [ ] Email campaign to pre-order list
- [ ] Influencer outreach (career coaches, HR people)
- [ ] Submit to app review blogs

## Content Ideas
1. "I'm building an AI interview coach - here's day 1"
2. "10 interview questions that make people panic"
3. "Why 90% of people fail technical interviews"
4. "Building a $3K MRR app in 90 days - live updates"
5. "AI gave me the best interview feedback I've ever received"

## Partnership Targets
- General Assembly (coding bootcamp)
- Career Karma (career platform)
- Pramp (interview practice platform)
- Local university career centers
- LinkedIn career coaches (search for hashtags)

## Metrics to Track
- Landing page conversion rate
- Social media engagement
- Pre-order revenue
- Email signups
- Referral traffic sources
